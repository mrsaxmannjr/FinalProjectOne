The Beginning

Building things has always interested me. Playing make believe as a child, bringing dolls to life was just the start. As I got older I started painting in abstract design. At age 20, I wrote a 120 page screenplay and incorporated four languages weaved throughout. Traveling to a myriad of places inspires my design. Being engulfed in business at Hewlett-Packard and Gartner gave me insight into what it takes to be a Fortune 20 and Fortune 50 company. Working for a British firm gave me international exposure on their strategy and the rollercoaster ride of expansion into new markets. I graduated with a B.A. in Public Relations from Bradley University (Illinois, USA). A Minnesota native, I moved to Arkansas after college for a job with Hewlett-Packard. I then relocated to Naples, Florida for a position with Gartner. I am currently located in the Denver metro studying software development at the Galvanize Platte campus. I am so happy in this field that I wake up before my alarm each morning. This community allows me to build, inspire others and create innovative products.
